Instructions

  1. Click top button to begin playing the first song.
  2. Click bottom button to pause the current song playing
  3. Click top button again to continue playing current song
  4. Click the right button to skip the current song and play the next song on the playlist.
  5. Above all, rock out to the beautiful masterpieces of Beethoven and Bach themselves.
